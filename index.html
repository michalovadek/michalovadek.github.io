Biography

I am a Lecturer (Assistant Professor) in European Institutions, Politics and Policy at University College London. Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Gothenburg in Sweden and a political advisor in the European Parliament. I obtained my PhD at KU Leuven in Belgium.

Most of my research addresses different aspects of the interplay between law and politics, frequently in the context of the European Union. I maintain the eurlex R package for accessing data on European Union law.
